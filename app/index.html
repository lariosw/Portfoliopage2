I am a creative person with a love for problem-solving. I am passionate about building unique web programs that make a difference in the user's life. I learn new skills quickly and can work on any topic or project that is thrown my way.



UX/UI
I design websites from start to finish. I begin with rough sketches, wireframes, sitemaps and comps that are focused on an optimal user experience with a stunning aesthetic design. Along with having a superb user experience my work is responsive to have the best experience on all device screen sizes.
